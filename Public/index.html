Digital Support Services: Cyber Security at Strode College

The course I am currently studying is a Level 3 T-Level in Digital Support Services: Cyber Security. This is a two year course that is equivalent to three A-Levels. The course is made up of 13 units, some of which are mandatory and some are optional. The mandatory units include; IT Systems, Computer Networks, Cyber Security, Technical Support, and Project Management. The optional units include; Website Development, Scripting, Mobile Device Management, and Cloud Services. The course is designed to give me a broad understanding of the digital support services industry, with a focus on cyber security. The course is also designed to give me the skills and knowledge needed to pursue a career in the digital support services industry or to continue my studies at university.
